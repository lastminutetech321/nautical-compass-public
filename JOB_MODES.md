# Job Modes

## Platform Dispatch
External coordinator uses the platform to fill labor.

## Contractor Mode
Internal operator secures contract and deploys labor from ledger.

## Referral Mode
Platform introduces worker/company/opportunity and charges referral or sourcing fee.

## Retained Relationship Mode
Platform maintains ongoing company relationship and charges recurring fee.

## Placement Protection Mode
System tracks introduced workers/relationships and applies staged fees if bypass occurs.
