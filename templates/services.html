Disputes & Billing

Payment & Billing Dispute Structuring
What it is: A clean dispute package that organizes the billing story with proof and next steps.
Best for: Incorrect charges, missing payouts, subscription issues, vendor billing conflicts.
Deliverable: Timeline + evidence checklist + dispute letter structure.
Chargeback & Trail Builder
What it is: A structured packet designed to support a chargeback or rebuttal with clean documentation.
Best for: Card disputes, merchant conflicts, “service not delivered,” partial delivery arguments.
Deliverable: Evidence map + recommended narrative + attachment list.

Contracts

Contract Review & Risk Mapping
What it is: Clause-by-clause risk scan that shows what matters, what’s missing, and where you’re exposed.
Best for: Production contracts, vendor terms, service agreements, partnerships, retainers.
Deliverable: Risk summary + redline guidance + “must-fix” list.
Demand Letter Drafting
What it is: A structured notice that states facts, requests remedy, and sets a deadline.
Best for: Breach situations, refunds, non-payment, contract violations, performance failures.
Deliverable: Ready-to-send letter + proof checklist.

Records & Requests

Record Requests (FOIA / Records Packets)
What it is: A properly formatted records request with the right scope and documentation plan.
Best for: Proof gathering, decision records, administrative trails, internal communications evidence.
Deliverable: Request letter + scope checklist + follow-up steps.
Dispute Timeline & Evidence Organization
What it is: A timeline that turns scattered events into a sequence that makes sense to any reviewer.
Best for: Any dispute where facts are messy, communications are scattered, or deadlines matter.
Deliverable: Timeline + evidence map + missing proof flags.

Platform / Marketplace Dispute Structuring

Deliverable: Escalation narrative + evidence bundle plan + risk flags.
Complaint Framing & Narrative Structuring
