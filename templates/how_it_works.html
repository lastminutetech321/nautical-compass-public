How It Works (Simple)

Think of the app like a control room: the intake captures facts, the system routes the request, and the dashboard shows the next move.

Step 1 — Choose a dashboard

Each role has its own view. No one sees a messy “everything page.”

  • Techs see gigs, rates, expectations.
  • Companies see requests, compliance, staffing.
  • Directors see rails, operators, scoreboards.

Step 2 — Intake captures facts

Intake removes human error and makes the next step obvious.

  • What happened?
  • What’s needed?
  • Deadline?
  • What outcome do you want?

Step 3 — System routes you

Routing rails decide what happens next (example outputs):

  • Send the user to the correct form / next page.
  • Generate a checklist.
  • Flag risks (ex: single customer concentration, missing contract, payment exposure).
  • Queue follow-up for staff/operator.
